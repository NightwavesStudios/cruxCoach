Train Smarter. Climb Harder.

Your personal climbing training companion – plan workouts, track progress, and discover exercises built for beinner and elite alike, progressing you over your entire climbing journey.

Personalized Training

Adaptive plans tailored to your skill level and climbing goals. Whether you're projecting your first V4 or chasing V10, CruxCoach adapts with you. Create 2-8 week plans catered around your needs and data or select a pre-made plan, both following a intensity cycle for peak performance and recovery.

Reflect on Sessions

Reflect on sessions to expose your strengths and weaknesses, update your data, and track your sessions through comments and other features long-term.

Analytics

Visualize where your spending your time, your strengths and weaknesses on holds, wall angles, and styles, your climbing style, your onsight, flash, redpoint and project level for all of your climbing types, and so much more.

Log Sessions

Log your climbing and training sessions, logging aspects like type, difficulty, median grade, and other customizable attributes to see reflected in your data, charts, training recommendations, and journal.

Explore Content

Find engaging lessons and workouts following a progressive structure allowing you to improve and delve deeper and intensify as you improve over time. Explore the content seperately or add them to you training plan to review and train later on a schedule.

Free, Forever

No subscriptions. No paywalls. No “14-day free trial” nonsense. CruxCoach is — and always will be — completely free. Why? Because we built this for climbers, not profit. Other apps felt limited or expensive, so we made something better — and decided to share it with the community.

About CruxCoach

CruxCoach originated from the need of an online journaling, tracking, analytics, and training platform for climbing, all in one. I found myself constantly switching from app to app that just barely met my needs and coping with the limited "free" tier. So...CruxCoach came to be. You can now journal, track, view analytics, and train for climbing all in one, for the sweet price of $0.
